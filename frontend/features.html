Enterprise-Grade Security

Your documents contain your most sensitive business intelligence. ENTRAG treats security as a first-class citizen. Every document is encrypted at rest and in transit, and access is controlled at a granular level.

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

    Admins define who can view, upload, and query which documents. Employees only see what they are authorized to see.

  • AES-256 Encryption at Rest

    All stored vectors and document chunks are encrypted using AES-256, the gold standard for enterprise data protection.

  • Full Audit Logs

    Every query, upload, and access event is timestamped and logged for compliance and forensic analysis.

  • SSO & JWT Authentication

    Integrates with Google Workspace, Microsoft Entra, Okta and more via standard OAuth 2.0.
