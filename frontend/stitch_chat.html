What are the specific requirements for parental leave according to the 2024 policy update?

Based on the 2024 Policy Update, parental leave requirements include:

  • Minimum of 12 months continuous service for full eligibility.
  • Formal notice must be submitted 10 weeks prior to the intended start date.
  • Total of 16 weeks paid leave, which can be split into two distinct blocks.
